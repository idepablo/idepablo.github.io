About

The story behind the data

For more than seven years I worked inside fintech operations at a payment processor, helping a portfolio of 12,000+ merchants keep running: analyzing their transactional, billing, and settlement data across $50M+ in monthly volume, resolving discrepancies, and building the reports leadership relied on to make decisions.

That work made me want to understand the tools and processes behind it all more deeply, so I went back to school for a B.S. in Information Technology, expanding my technical foundation and learning how the systems I had worked with actually fit together.

Along the way I realized my real passion was in the data itself. I leaned all the way in: an intensive Data Analytics Bootcamp, a growing portfolio of real-world SQL, Python, Tableau, and Power BI projects, and an incoming M.S. in Data Analytics, always sharpening how I turn messy data into decisions. It is a craft I plan to keep enriching for a long time.
