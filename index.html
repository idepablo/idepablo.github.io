About Me

The story behind the data

I'm Ignacio, a data analyst based in Seattle with 7+ years of experience in fintech operations. I've spent those years working closely with transactional data, building reports that informed leadership decisions, and identifying patterns that drove real operational change.

That experience taught me something important: the value of data isn't in the numbers themselves — it's in communicating what those numbers mean to the people who need to act on them. I'm now channeling that foundation into a full transition into data analytics, completing both a B.S. in IT and a hands-on Data Analytics bootcamp.

🎯 Actively seeking Data Analyst and Business Intelligence roles where I can combine deep fintech domain knowledge with a growing technical toolkit to drive data-informed decisions.

Outside of work, I'm an active fencer, a cinema enthusiast, and an amateur space nerd — and above all, a family man.
