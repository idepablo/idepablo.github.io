About Me

The story behind the data

I'm Ignacio, a data analyst based in Seattle. For 7+ years I worked inside fintech operations at a payment processing company, managing a portfolio of 12,000+ merchants, building financial reports that informed leadership decisions, and analyzing transactional data to identify revenue leakage and operational risk across $50M+ in monthly settlement volume.

That work taught me the most valuable skill in data: understanding what decision a stakeholder needs to make, and building the analysis backwards from there. I'm now transitioning fully into data analytics, completing both a B.S. in IT and a hands-on Data Analytics Bootcamp while building a project portfolio across Excel, SQL, Tableau, Power BI, and Python.

🎯 Actively seeking Data Analyst and Business Intelligence roles where I can apply both fintech domain expertise and growing technical skills to drive data-informed decisions.

Outside of work, I love practicing fencing, watching good cinema, being an amateur space nerd, and spending time with my family.
